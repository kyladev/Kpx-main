Resources - Exploits & Guides

WARNING: I TAKE NO RESPONSIBILITY IF YOU DAMAGE OR DESTROY YOUR COMPUTER USING THESE EXPLOITS. TAKE CAUTION WHEN USING THESE.

Executing BAT Files

This lets you run BAT files on an unelevated level, even when blocked, even allowing CMD. Tested on Windows 10 and 11.

Alternate OS Boot

This lets you boot another OS of your choice onto your computer. This is usually blocked. Result and proccess vary by your computer manufactuer.

Rewrite Group Policy

This lets you rewrite the group policy on your computer, letting EXE files and other blocked applications run.

Unlock BIOS Settings

This lets you unlock the BIOS options on your computer, which lets you change startup options. Only works with some BIOS versions.

Methods to Access CMD

These are various ways of getting a cmd prompt, with varying levels of success depending on your computer. Made for Windows 10 and 11.
